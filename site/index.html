The right amount of detail

A little utility you’ll use all day.

Browse nearby days, choose exactly which calendars matter, and let UpNext start with your Mac. When an update is ready, it appears gently in the same place.

  • Live countdowns for upcoming meetings
  • Clear active, imminent, and later states
  • Calendar filtering and day navigation
  • Signed, notarized automatic updates
⌂

Private means local

Your meetings are none of our business.

UpNext reads calendar events through macOS EventKit and processes them entirely on your Mac. Network access is used only to retrieve signed software updates from GitHub.
